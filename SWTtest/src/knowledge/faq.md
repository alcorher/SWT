# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

## General Questions

### How far in advance should I book?
For group tours, we recommend booking at least a few days in advance. For private tours, please contact us directly at info@sevillawalkingtours.com or call +34 616 501100.

### What is included in the tour price?
The tour price typically includes the guided walking tour. Some tours have optional add-ons for entrance tickets to monuments. Please check the specific tour description for details.

### Are entrance tickets included?
Most of our tours are available in two options:
- **Without tickets:** Lower price, you can purchase tickets on arrival or in advance
- **With tickets:** Tickets included for your convenience

Specific tours with ticket options are clearly indicated on the booking page.

### What's the maximum group size?
Unlike typical "small groups" of 30+ people, we pride ourselves on truly small, intimate groups for a more personal experience and better guide-to-visitor interaction.

### Are children allowed on tours?
Yes! We offer dedicated Family Tours designed for children and parents. Other group tours may also be suitable - please contact us to discuss your specific group composition.

### What if I have mobility concerns?
Please contact us directly to discuss your specific needs. Some tours may require more walking than others. We'll help you find the best option.

## What to Expect

### What should I wear?
- **Comfortable walking shoes** - You'll be walking through Seville's historic streets
- **Weather-appropriate clothing** - Check seasonal weather forecasts
- **Sun protection** - Hat and sunscreen recommended, especially in summer
- **Water bottle** - Bring your own or purchase one during the tour

### How long are the tours?
Tour durations vary:
- **Intro tours:** Typically 2-3 hours
- **Extended tours:** 4-6 hours
- **Day trips:** Full day (8+ hours)

Contact us for specific tour duration information.

### What time do tours depart?
Tour times vary by season and tour type. Check the specific tour page or contact us for current schedules.

### Can I customize a tour?
Absolutely! We specialize in private, tailor-made tours. Contact us to discuss your interests and we'll create a perfect itinerary.
