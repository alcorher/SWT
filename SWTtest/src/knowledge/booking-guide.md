# Booking Guide

## How to Reserve Your Sevilla Walking Tour

### Step 1: Choose Your Tour Type
We offer three main categories:

**Group Tours**
- Join other travelers for authentic small group experiences
- Various themes: history, culture, food, architecture
- Typically 4-12 people maximum

**Private Tours**
- Customize your experience entirely
- Perfect for families, special occasions, corporate groups
- Create your perfect Seville itinerary

**Day Trips**
- Full-day adventures outside Seville
- Ronda and Cádiz/Jerez options available
- Includes transportation and guide

### Step 2: Select Your Specific Tour

**Popular Group Tours:**
- Sevilla City Intro Tour
- Cathedral and Alcázar Tour
- Cathedral, Alcázar and Santa Cruz Quarter
- Triana to Plaza de España
- Tapas Tour Off the Beaten Path
- Family Tour

**Day Trip Options:**
- Day Trip to Ronda
- Day Trip to Cádiz and Jerez

### Step 3: Decide on Entrance Tickets
Most tours come in two options:
- **Tickets NOT included:** Save money if you have Seville cards or prefer DIY
- **Tickets included:** Convenient all-in-one pricing with entrance fees

### Step 4: Book Online or Contact Us

**Book Online:**
- Visit: https://www.sevillawalkingtours.com/group-tours-and-activities
- Select your tour date and number of participants
- Complete booking through website

**Contact Direct:**
- **Phone:** +34 616 501100
- **Email:** info@sevillawalkingtours.com
- Speak with our team for personalized recommendations or private tour requests

### Step 5: Confirmation
You'll receive booking confirmation with:
- Tour date and time
- Meeting location
- What to bring
- Contact information
- Any special instructions
